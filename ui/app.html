Real Estate News & Trends

Overview

Bengaluru, known as the Silicon Valley of India, has a thriving real estate market. The city offers a mix of affordable housing, luxury apartments, and commercial spaces.

Current Trends

  • Demand: The demand for 2BHK and 3BHK apartments is on the rise due to the influx of IT professionals.
  • Hot Locations: Electronic City, Whitefield, and Sarjapur Road are among the most sought-after areas.
  • Market Update: Property prices have seen a steady increase of 5-7% over the past year.
